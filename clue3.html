Diner Receipt

Night Owl Diner, 1:13 AM. Coffee, black. One slice of pie, untouched—the total includes it, but there’s no frosting smudge on the paper.

The receipt heat-ink is fresh; it curls at the corner where something wet touched it, then dried. The server’s name is crossed out with a single line. Why?

Scrawled on the back: “Tracks split. Red door.”
