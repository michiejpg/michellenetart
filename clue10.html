Hotel Keycard

The magnetic strip is scratched, but the number embossed along the bottom is clear: 407. The same ghosted number from the bus ticket.

On the white edge, barely there, an imprint left by a cuff button—round, chipped enamel, red.

On the back, a hairline of rust-colored dust. Like the paint from a door that shouldn’t have been opened.
