Bus Ticket

Riverside, one-way, 2:05 AM. The punch is clean, but the ink bled at the edges—printed by a machine overdue for maintenance.

On the back: a faint number indented into the paper, as if written on the sheet above it. Pressed hard enough to leave a ghost: 407.

The corner is torn not by accident but along the same angle each time. A signal between people who know what to look for.
